About Me

At AWS I worked on a team of data scientists responsible for researching operational data of Amazon's web services. I synthesized complex data, developed success metrics, measured output and automation, and employed predictive analytics through experimentation, machine learning, etc.

Prior to AWS, I progressed through junior, mid, to senior scientist at Booz Allen where I interacted with clients weekly, helped with strategic planning of tech solutions, and delivered tactical work including product data analytics, ETL, and modeling. During my tenure there, I supported or led multiple high impact projects with groups of talented team members that resulted in contract renewals and work captures valued in the millions of dollars.

I enjoy cross-functional leadership roles, guiding teams and stakeholders on product innovations powered by data, and mentoring junior team members. Because I have a broad skillset, I am focused on going deep and specializing in Natural Language Processing (NLP) and Large Language Models (LLMs).
